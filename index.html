About Mrs. Tiphany Richardson

Tiphany Richardson is a wife, mother, entrepreneur, and a early childhood educator; who aspires to make a positive impact in the profession of early childhood education, Mrs. Richardson leads Creative Christian Preschool & Learning Center, INC. with a heart for faith-driven education, joyful rigor, and nurturing every child’s potential .

Our mission

To partner with families in building character, curiosity, and academic confidence—preparing children to serve, lead, and thrive.

Our approach

Professional standards with playful energy: structured foundations in reading and math, hands-on projects, and warm community.
